Fill out the boxes. Who will you marry? How will you get around?

When you're ready, press and hold the pink button. Hearts will appear. The number of hearts that appear before you choose to release the button will be your magic number.

The game will then count out each item and when it gets to the magic number, it'll cross out that option and start the counting over until the final option is chosen for each category. You can also play this game with good ol' paper and pencil.
